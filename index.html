Join  us  to make it a better place
What?
We are a group of volunteers who want to change the way Bangaloreans think about litter, primarily through clean-ups or "trash mobs".
Why?
We are a group of volunteers who want to change the way Bangaloreans think about litter, primarily through clean-ups, trash mobs.
How?
 For these trash mobs, we select a public space, usually a plot of land or area that has a significant amount of litter, and we set a time and day to go and pick up trash there. They usually are scheduled about once a week on Sunday evenings.

We welcome community feedback on which areas of South Bangalore are heavily-littered that we may target for future trash mobs. 

If you would like to join our email list, message us with your email address, or contact us at trashmob2015@gmail.com! We send out emails about once a week with info on upcoming mobs and other clean-ups in the southern part of the city.
It's our country, our city, our locality, our home.  We need to be the change to see a change. Join us in achieving a cleaner, greener, better Bangalore.
